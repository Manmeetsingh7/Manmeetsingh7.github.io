About Our Organization

Planet Pure is a student-created organization focused on raising awareness about plastic pollution. We aim to educate people about the dangers of single-use plastics and inspire sustainable habits.

Our Activities

We organize local clean-up events, share educational articles and blog posts, and provide tips for reducing plastic use in daily life. Our goal is to make a positive impact on the environment.
